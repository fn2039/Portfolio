A Day in Quarantine Life

The whole film and the website revolves around the idea of quarantine and mental health. Due to prevailing circumstances, institutes have adopted the online education model. During the 30 minutes film festival activity, the first idea a group of four strangers agreed was about quarantine and its struggles. In this project, the whole team share their perspective on quarantine life.

A Pilot's Journey

A Pilot's Journey is an interactive comic on web that talks about how some nations are handling Covid19 situation and its effects on the nation in a lighthearted way. The intention is not at all to joke about the situation, but for sarcastically make people realize their wrong actions at wrong times and giving them an insight on how badly many places have been affected.

The Missing Child

This is a sound project focused on a mysterious kidnapping and murder. The sound piece aims to trigger audiences’ wild imagination through abstraction, i.e without explicit mention of place, characters and plot. Catch the subtle hints and create a highly individualized experience. This project is of interest to, specifically, suspense, horror, mystery, murder, and triller lovers.

LSFR - The Box

Due to pandemic, many people struggled with their friendships. The project is about branding an AI integrated device that is going to be users’ friend. The device can be customized to users' preference in terms of model, color and language. This idea is being conveyed through an advertisement, where the customized products are being used by three different users in their daily life.
